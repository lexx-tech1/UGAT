WHO WE ARE

About UGAT

UGAT Integrated Farm is a community-based training center in San Isidro, Daet, Camarines Norte dedicated to sustainable agriculture and livelihood empowerment.

🌱

Our Mission

To empower local communities through hands-on agricultural training, equipping individuals with the skills and knowledge needed for sustainable farming and food security.

🔭

Our Vision

A community where every household practices sustainable agriculture, with access to training, certification, and a reliable farm product marketplace.

About TrainTrack

UGAT TrainTrack is the official management system of UGAT Integrated Farm. It centralizes all trainee records, workshop scheduling, session attendance, certificate issuance, and farm product inventory in one digital platform.

The system bridges admin staff and trainees — admins manage operations while trainees track their own progress, certifications, and shop for farm products anytime.

  • ✅ Paperless enrollment and attendance
  • ✅ Automated certificate eligibility tracking
  • ✅ SMS notifications via Semaphore API
  • ✅ Live inventory alerts for kits and farm products
