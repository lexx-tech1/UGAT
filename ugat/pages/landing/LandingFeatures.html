WHAT WE OFFER

System Features

Everything you need to manage agricultural training, from enrollment to certification — all in one platform.

👥

Trainee Management

Register and store complete trainee profiles including personal info, educational background, classification (4Ps, OFW, TESDA alumni, etc.), and ID photos. Search, filter, and export records anytime.

📋

Workshop Management

Create workshops with multiple sessions, set dates, assign facilitators, and manage slot availability. Trainees can browse and enroll directly through the system.

✅

Attendance Tracking

Log session attendance as Present, Late, or Absent per trainee. View attendance rates, filter by workshop or date range, and export reports in one click.

🏆

Certifications

The system automatically identifies trainees who completed all required sessions. Admins issue certificates with a single click — triggering an SMS confirmation to the trainee via Semaphore.

🌾

Farm Shop

Trainees can browse and order farm products and training kits directly through the system. Includes category filtering, stock status badges, and add-to-cart functionality.

📦

Inventory Management

Track all farm products and training kit stock levels. Get low-stock alerts before session days so you never run short on kits for enrolled trainees.

📱

SMS Notifications

Automatic SMS messages are sent to trainees upon enrollment confirmation and certificate issuance using the Semaphore API — no manual texting needed.

📊

Reports & Export

Export attendance records, trainee lists, and certificate logs. Designed to support TESDA compliance and other government reporting requirements.
